Welcome to Central Expanded Metal

Established in 1985, Central Expanded Metal, Inc. is an innovative expanded metal manufacturer, supplying engineered products to customers across North America. We pride ourselves in our commitment to customer satisfaction. Working in conjunction with both our production staff and sales department, our full-time customer service representatives stand ready to meet your needs. Central Expanded Metal provides the service you deserve with a dedication you will appreciate.
Free from scrap and raveling, expanded metal can be expanded up to ten times its original size, allowing it to be a less costly alternative to perforated metal and wire cloth. The ability to engineer and fabricate expanded metal to any specific application yields a product uniquely suited to every end-use. Expanded metal can lose between 75%-80% of its weight and becomes stronger and more rigid than its previous state. Feel free to contact us for additional information and product pricing.
