About Us

Central Expanded Metal, Inc. is an innovator in expanded metal manufacturing, supplying quality products to satisfied customers across North America. We pride ourselves in our commitment to customer satisfaction. We stand ready to work with you to fabricate your own made-to-order parts. Central Expanded Metal provides the service you deserve with a dedication you will appreciate. Our experienced personnel produce full-sized sheets, custom-made parts, and coiled expanded metal from our 90,000 square-foot facility in Chandler, Oklahoma. We possess many general fabricating capabilities to help lower your overall costs. As one of the leading suppliers to the original equipment manufacturers (OEM), we are always ready to discuss any requirements you might have.
