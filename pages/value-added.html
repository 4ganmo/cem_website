Solid Steel Border Can Be Created on Sides

The Step-feature allows Central Expanded Metal to include a solid border or strip along the LWD of the item. This process increases the cost of the material, but can cut welding and fabrication costs in half, while providing a safer edge to the part. Streamline your production efficiency by working with us to engineer your new part.

U-Edging Accessories

U-Edging is a popular option when finish framing is desired. Manufactured from 18 gauge carbon steel, the u-edging comes in 1" widths with openings to accommodate various sheet thicknesses. Let us attach the edging for you via press-fit or welding.
