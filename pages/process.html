Process

Although there are standard designs, expanded metal is infinitely variable in pattern, and can be specified to achieve any critical characteristics. A larger pattern size will increase open area, light, sound diffusion, and cost-effectiveness. Changing the strand width alters appearances and weight. In fact, expanded metal actually has a higher strength to weight ratio than in its previous sheet metal state.
