Architectural Patterns

The following expanded metal patterns have all been used in the architectural and design industry. Central Expanded Metal understands the strict requirements of architectural mesh and will work with the customer to establish exact requirements for material type and tolerances that best fit the consumers needs.
For custom patterns and specific request please call or email Central Expanded Metal.

The AE pattern below has a distinctive arc-shaped SWD. 1.200" LWD x .525" SWD

AC pattern has a similar hex-shape, but is larger than E pattern. The SWD can be enlarged. .750" LWD x .180" minimum SWD
